Events

Annual Events

  • During January we have a CAP reunion that serves as an annual gathering of MAACS/CAP Alumni and current CAP students in Bridgeport. Donors and Board Members are invited to catch up with students and see the progress they have all made. A handful of attending students are recognized for their hard work and perseverance.
  • In May we honor amazing educators and leaders in Bridgeport at the Inspiration Awards Event. Usually eight (8) outstanding teachers who demonstrate leadership in education. Each recipient receives a Tiffany Crystal Apple and a $1,000 certificate entitling them to professional development or for classroom/student enrichment. The Theodore & Margaret Beard Award is given to honor two (2) Bridgeport teachers for their great instructional skills, and passion for student inspiration. Each receives a $20,000 gift payable over 3 years. The Elizabeth Pfriem recipient will receive a handcrafted golden apple lapel pin. It was designed as a special award to honor one of the founders of the BPEF, Elizabeth Pfriem. Last but not least the George Bellinger Award is presented to a principal in the Bridgeport Public Schools who best replicates the ability of George Bellinger to foster & recognize good leadership. The First Inspiration Award was held in 1993.
  • Towards the end of Spring, students from Bassick, Central and Fairchild Wheeler Magnet High School who were in MAACS for a minimum of two (2) years will attend Housatonic Community College for a one day CAP Workshop. At the Workshop, students will engage with one another while working with college professors and volunteers. This program was created in 2003 to ensure that MAACS graduates who were admitted to college graduate from college.
